Shwarma

Description

Shwarma is a delicious Middle Eastern dish made with marinated meat (typically chicken, lamb, or beef), wrapped in pita bread or served with rice. It is often accompanied by tahini sauce, vegetables, and pickles.

Ingredients

  • Marinated chicken, lamb, or beef
  • Pita bread or rice
  • Tahini sauce
  • Vegetables (lettuce, tomatoes, cucumbers)
  • Pickles
  • Garlic sauce (optional)

Steps

  1. Prepare the marinated meat by grilling or roasting until cooked thoroughly.
  2. Warm the pita bread or cook the rice according to package instructions.
  3. Slice the cooked meat into thin strips.
  4. Assemble the shwarma by placing the meat, vegetables, and sauces on the pita bread or rice.
  5. Wrap the filled pita bread or serve the shwarma with rice on a plate.
  6. Enjoy your delicious homemade shwarma!
