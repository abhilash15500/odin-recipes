Pizza

Description

Pizza is a popular Italian dish consisting of a yeasted flatbread typically topped with tomato sauce, cheese, and various toppings. It is baked in an oven and enjoyed worldwide.

Ingredients

  • Pizza dough
  • Tomato sauce
  • Cheese (mozzarella, parmesan)
  • Toppings (pepperoni, mushrooms, bell peppers, onions, olives, etc.)
  • Herbs and spices (basil, oregano, garlic powder)

Steps

  1. Preheat your oven to the desired temperature (typically around 475°F or 245°C).
  2. Roll out the pizza dough on a floured surface to your desired thickness.
  3. Spread tomato sauce evenly over the dough, leaving a border for the crust.
  4. Sprinkle shredded cheese over the sauce, then add your desired toppings.
  5. Bake the pizza in the preheated oven for 10-15 minutes or until the crust is golden brown and the cheese is bubbly.
  6. Remove from the oven, slice, and serve hot.
  7. Enjoy your homemade pizza!
