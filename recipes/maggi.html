Maggi

Description

Maggi is a popular instant noodle dish that originated in Switzerland but gained immense popularity in India and other parts of the world. It is quick and easy to prepare, making it a favorite among college students and busy individuals.

Ingredients

  • Maggi noodles
  • Maggi masala seasoning (included in the packet)
  • Water
  • Vegetables (optional, such as carrots, peas, bell peppers)

Steps

  1. Boil water in a saucepan.
  2. Once the water is boiling, add the Maggi noodles and cook for 2 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  3. Add the Maggi masala seasoning and stir well.
  4. If desired, add chopped vegetables and cook for an additional 2 minutes.
  5. Remove from heat and let it sit for a minute to allow the flavors to meld.
  6. Transfer to a bowl and serve hot.
  7. Enjoy your quick and delicious Maggi!
